About me

Hello! I'm a second year PhD student in the Institute of Astronomy, at the University of Cambridge, where I spend my time researching Galaxy dynamics! I mostly specialise in the dynamics of the stellar halo, and it's interactions with the Large Magellanic Cloud, dark matter subhaloes and the Galactic bar.

I grew up in North Wales, where I lived until I was 18. I moved away from home when I was inspired and helped by the Sutton Trust US Programme to attended and ultimately accept a place at Princeton University in the United States. Following this, I spent one year at the University of Edinburgh, and then eventually was fortunate to be accepted onto a PhD program at the Institute of Astronomy.

Apart from research, I spend my time doing outreach, teaching, pub-quizzing, watching movies, playing video games and occasionally reading!
