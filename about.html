About me

My name is Elliot, I'm a second year PhD student in the Institute of Astronomy, at the University of Cambridge, where I spent my time researching Galaxy dynamics. I grew up in North Wales, where I lived until I was 18. I moved away from home when I was inspired and helped by the Sutton Trust US Programme to attended and ultimately accept a place at Princeton University in the United States on a full-ride scholarship. Following this, I spent one year at the University of Edinburgh, and then eventually was fortunate to be accepted onto a PhD program.
